// How It Works

Built on your stack.
Owned by you.

No new dashboards. No vendor lock‑in. Q0 plugs into what you already run — Gmail, Google Apps Script, and your PSA.

01

Ticket Arrives

Email hits your Gmail inbox. Google Apps Script triggers automatically — no polling, no delay.

02

AI Classifies

OpenAI or Claude API reads the ticket body, assigns priority (P1–P4), category, and the right technician.

03

PSA Receives

Classified ticket routes to ConnectWise, Autotask, or your custom PSA — fully tagged and assigned.

04

You Own It

The code lives in your Google account. No external SaaS. No monthly platform fee. Full control, forever.
