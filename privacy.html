Privacy Policy

Effective: March 27, 2026

Overview

Q0 is a deployment service operated by Evera48 LLC. We build AI-powered ticket triage systems that run entirely within your infrastructure. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data We Don't Collect

Q0 is deployed inside your Google Workspace and PSA environment. Once deployed:

  • We do not access, store, or process your ticket data
  • We do not log API calls made from your Apps Script
  • We do not install telemetry, analytics, or tracking in your deployment
  • We are not a proxy or relay — your systems call AI APIs directly

Data We Do Collect

During the sales and deployment process, we may collect:

  • Contact information — name and email address submitted via our waitlist or contact form
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe; we never see or store your full card number
  • Deployment configuration — technical details about your PSA and email setup needed to build your deployment

How We Use Your Data

Contact information is used solely to communicate about your deployment or waitlist status. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.

Third-Party Services

Our website and payment processing use:

  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security
  • Stripe — payment processing

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data handling.

Data Retention

Waitlist emails are retained until you request removal. Deployment configuration details are deleted 30 days after project completion. Payment records are retained as required by law.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. We will respond within 30 days.
